So defeated GM Praggnanandhaa Rameshbabu of India for the second straight round, while Magnus Carlsen extracted revenge against GM Alireza Firouzja of France, who had led the 14-player double round-robin since the opening round. GM Vincent Keymer of Germany also claimed his first win of the event — in either classical play or armageddon (a tiebreak format where Black has draw odds with less thinking time) — by defeating World Champion Gukesh Dommaraju. In the parallel Women's section, Women's World Champion Ju Wenjun of China ended a five-match losing streak in armageddon by beating GM Divya Deshmukh of India in a classical thriller, allowing GM Bibisara Assaubayeva of Kazakhstan to reclaim the sole women's lead."
