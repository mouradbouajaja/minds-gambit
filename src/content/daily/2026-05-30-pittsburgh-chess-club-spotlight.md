Regular members get discounted entry to the club's Tuesday-night events — most recently the Wild Card Open, a six-round Swiss at Game/90 — along with access to classes, lectures, and the club's book library. The club is also the home organizer of the annual Pittsburgh Open, the city's largest event, which returns July 17–19 with $13,000 in guaranteed prizes."
