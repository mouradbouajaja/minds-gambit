summary: "FIDE, the international chess federation that governs world-title competition, has announced Hong Kong's Queen Elizabeth Stadium as the host venue for the 2026 World Rapid & Blitz Team Chess Championships, running June 17 to 21. The team event pits nations against one another in shortened time controls — rapid (15-minute games with 10-second increments) and blitz (3-minute games with 2-second increments) — and is one of FIDE's flagship multi-day events outside the world title cycle. Federations from every continent are confirmed, with the United States, Norway, India, China, and the host Hong Kong all sending top-rated lineups."
