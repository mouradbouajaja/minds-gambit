summary: "FIDE has confirmed the full team lineups for the 2026 World Team Rapid and Blitz Chess Championships, to be held June 17–21 at Queen Elizabeth Stadium in Hong Kong, in what will be the event's first appearance in East Asia. Defending blitz champions Team WR Chess — featuring the rare pairing of Magnus Carlsen and Fabiano Caruana on the same squad, alongside GM Wesley So — will face stiff competition from Hexamind, whose roster includes GM Alireza Firouzja and GM Levon Aronian, and from the Uzbekistan (Central Asian nation bordering Kazakhstan and Afghanistan) national side, headlined by World Championship Challenger GM Javokhir Sindarov. A total of 43 teams and more than 300 players are confirmed for the €500,000 event, which requires each team to include both a female player and a recreational player who has never reached a FIDE rating of 2000."
